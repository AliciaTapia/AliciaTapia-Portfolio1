About Me

I was born and have spent most of my life in Monterrey, Mexico. I studied chemical engineering with a focus on the food industry at the ITESM. My professional experience has been varied across several leadership positions as a portfolio manager and financial manager for both large (two of Mexico’s top 10) and small, 30 person, companies. Additionally, I have worked as a program manager for a nonprofit organization. I moved to Houston, TX in the summer of 2015 with my family. I enjoy reading and staying current on local and world politics, traveling and yoga.

When I moved to the U.S., I knew I was ready for a career change. I chose a certification as a Full stack developer by UT Austin because I enjoy all the challenges a developer faces. I like to work with different teams, learn new conepts, technologies and be constantly challenged. I am interested in looking for opportunities that will allow me to apply what I have learned and to continue to grow in my professional career, be that in a corporate or start-up environment, as I know there are always opportunities to grow as a professional and contribute to a successful team environment regardless of the size of a company.

Please take a minute to check out and enjoy some of the recent stuff I have been working on. And, of course, I would love to hear from you so don’t be afraid of reaching out to contact me.
